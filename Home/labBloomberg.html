Bloomberg Terminals

The Sklarsky Center for Business Analytics (LOW-1225) has 12 Bloomberg Terminals that may be used by students, faculty and staff.

To access the Bloomberg Terminals, sign into one of the workstations with your RIT Computer Account. Then open Bloomberg and login with your Bloomberg account. If you do not have a Bloomberg account, setup an account.
